Bike to Work Checklist

Bike to Work Checklist:

  • Work things.
    • Bag (rain cover).
    • Computer (mouse, mousepad, keyboard, charger).
    • Headphones (charging cables).
    • Camera (batteries, cables, charger).
    • Phone (charger).
    • Wallet.
    • Keys.
    • Water bottle.
    • Lunch (lunch box, utensils).
    • Supplements.
  • Bike-specific things.
    • Front light.
    • Back light.
    • Bike lock.
    • Hand pump.
    • Multi-tool.
    • 2x inner tube.
    • Patch kit.
    • Tire levers.
  • Clothing
    • Extra shirt.
    • Jacket.
  • Deodorant.
  • Sun screen.
  • Facial routine (facial wash, toner, serum, moisturizer).
  • Wet wipes.

Before leaving house:

  • Check brakes.
  • Check shifting.
  • Check water.
  • Lock house.
