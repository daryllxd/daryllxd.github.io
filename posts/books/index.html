Good Habits: Reading Books

Post objective: To get people to read more books.

When I was a kid I really liked to read books. I’m just that kind of nerd. Books were my favortie past time. I remember waiting every weekend when my mom, Jerico, and I would go out during the weekends and she would buy me 1 book. I efven finished ROald Dahl and shit.

Then came video games and I was just addicted to them. They wree a higher stimulus than books.

But recently I want to read books again. It all started when I was thinking to myself, I want to get better at making conversations. I want to have actionable things on how to be better at this because Iwant to get my ideas out. Not just in terms of technical terms . It started with How to Win Friends & Influence People. Now I’ve read 15 books over the past 5 months.
